What is a Deepfake?

A "deepfake" is a term for synthetic media created using artificial intelligence (AI). By combining and superimposing existing images and videos onto source images or videos (using a technique known as a generative adversarial network, or GAN), AI can create realistic-looking fake media that is often indistinguishable from the real thing.

While this technology can be used for harmless fun, it is increasingly used for malicious purposes, such as creating fake news, spreading disinformation, committing fraud, or creating non-consensual explicit imagery. TrueSight is built to help you identify and combat this threat.
